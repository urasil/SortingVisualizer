Quest="In which case it would be best use mergesort rather than another sorting algorithm?"
Image=""
Choice_1="List already sorted"
Choice_2="Sorting a linked list"
Choice_3="Sorting very large datasets"
Choice_4="Sorting very small datasets"
Answer="2"
Explanation="Mergesort is good at sorting a linked list because it doesn't require random access to elements, and its divide-and-conquer approach efficiently handles the merging of smaller sublists."
